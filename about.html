About us

Welcome to Carenetics Health care System, the healthcare monitoring system that uses advanced sensors to measure weight, BMI, and other important health metrics. Our goal is to provide you with the tools you need to track your health in real-time, so you can make informed decisions and improve your wellbeing.

About Our Project

Our project uses advanced sensors to measure different metrics related to a person's health and fitness, including weight, body mass index (BMI), and oxygen levels. By combining these measurements, we can provide users with a more complete picture of their overall health and help them track their progress over time.

Our sensors

We basically use three kinds of sensor which is pluse sensor, load sensor, oxygen sensor. The pluse sensor measures a user's heart rate in real time, which can help them monitor their cardiovascular health and track their workouts.The load sensor measures a user's weight, allowing us to calculate their BMI and track changes over time.

Our Goals

Our project aims to make it easier for people to monitor their health and fitness, without the need for expensive equipment or frequent doctor visits. By providing users with accurate and actionable data, we hope to empower them to take control of their health and make informed decisions about their lifestyle and habits.

Our Team

Our team is made up of experienced engineers and healthcare professionals who are passionate about using technology to improve people's lives. We believe that everyone deserves access to high-quality healthcare, and we're committed to making that a reality through our work on this project.
